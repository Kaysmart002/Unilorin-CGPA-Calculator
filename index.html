About The Calculator


This Calculator is used for Calculating CGPA of a school with 5.00 grading system like Unilorin

HOW IT WORKS

Step 1: Fill in your Course code, Course Unit and Select your Grade.
Step 2: Click on Add course to add other courses.
Step 3: Remove Course if necessary.
Step 4: Click on Calculate CGPA and view your CGPA.
